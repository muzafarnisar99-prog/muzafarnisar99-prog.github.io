🏷️ About Monobenzone Cream

Monobenzone cream (monobenzyl ether of hydroquinone / MBEH) is an FDA-approved topical depigmenting agent used exclusively for permanent depigmentation in patients with extensive vitiligo. It has been FDA-approved since 1952 and is the most potent depigmenting agent available.

Our monobenzone cream is manufactured by Behvazan Pharmaceutical Co. (Rasht, Iran) and contains the same active ingredient as the well-known Benoquin cream — available in 20% and 40% concentrations.

⚖️ Monobenzone in USA
