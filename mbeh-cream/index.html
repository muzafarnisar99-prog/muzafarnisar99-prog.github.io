What is MBEH Cream?

MBEH stands for Monobenzyl Ether of Hydroquinone — the chemical name for monobenzone. MBEH cream is pharmaceutical-grade monobenzone topical cream used for permanent depigmentation in vitiligo patients.

MBEH (monobenzone) is classified as the most potent depigmenting agent available, and has been FDA-approved since 1952 for permanent depigmentation of extensive vitiligo.

MBEH / Monobenzone — Same Product

The following terms all refer to the same pharmaceutical product:

  • MBEH cream — chemical abbreviation
  • Monobenzone cream — generic pharmaceutical name
  • Monobenzyl ether of hydroquinone cream — full chemical name
  • Benoquin cream — discontinued brand name
  • Monozan cream — Behvazan Pharmaceutical brand name

How MBEH Works

MBEH causes free-radical-mediated destruction of melanocytes and inhibits tyrosinase activity — permanently removing pigment from treated skin areas. The effect is irreversible as destroyed melanocytes cannot regenerate.
