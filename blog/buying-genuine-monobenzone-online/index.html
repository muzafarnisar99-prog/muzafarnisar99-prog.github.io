With monobenzone cream being a high-value pharmaceutical product, the market unfortunately contains counterfeit and substandard products. This guide helps you identify genuine monobenzone cream, understand what to look for, and order safely from legitimate international suppliers.

Benoquin vs Monozan — Recognizing Authentic Creams

The two most well-known pharmaceutical brands for monobenzone cream are:

Benoquin (ICN/Valeant Pharmaceuticals)

Benoquin was the original FDA-approved brand for monobenzone 20% cream. Important: Benoquin has been discontinued and is no longer manufactured. Any product sold as "original Benoquin" in 2024–2026 is either old stock (check expiry carefully) or mislabeled. Be cautious.

Monozan (Behvazan Pharmaceutical Co.)

Monozan is manufactured by Behvazan Pharmaceutical Co. in Rasht, Iran and is the most widely available pharmaceutical-grade monobenzone cream currently in production. Authentic Monozan packaging features:

  • Green and white box design
  • Clear text: "Monobenzone Cream USP 40%" or "20%"
  • "For External Use Only" prominently displayed
  • "Extemporaneous Preparation" notation
  • 30gm tube clearly labeled
  • Manufacturer details on the box
  • Lot number and expiry date printed on both box and tube
✅ We supply genuine Monozan cream by Behvazan Pharmaceutical Co. Every order includes the original manufacturer packaging with lot number, expiry date, and Certificate of Analysis.

Avoiding Counterfeits — What to Look For

Red flags that indicate a product may be counterfeit or substandard:

  • No manufacturer name or address on packaging
  • No lot number or expiry date
  • Suspiciously low price (below $8 per tube)
  • Poor print quality on packaging — blurry text or faded colors
  • Seller cannot provide Certificate of Analysis
  • Product description vague or inconsistent
  • No clear country of manufacture stated

Always verify that the active ingredient listed is monobenzone (monobenzyl ether of hydroquinone) at the stated concentration (20% or 40%).

⚕️ Using counterfeit or substandard monobenzone cream is dangerous. Incorrect concentration or impure ingredients can cause unexpected skin reactions. Always buy from verified pharmaceutical suppliers.

Shipping Tips: Discreet, Legal and International

When ordering monobenzone cream internationally:

  • Use established couriers — UPS and FedEx international offer tracking, customs clearance support, and reliable delivery
  • Request full documentation — commercial invoice, packing list, and Certificate of Analysis for smooth customs clearance
  • Check import regulations for your country — prescription requirements vary. We can advise on documentation needed for your specific country
  • Discreet packaging — we ship in plain packaging without product names visible on the outside
  • Tracking — all shipments include full UPS or FedEx tracking numbers
