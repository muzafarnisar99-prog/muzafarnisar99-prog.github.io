What is Monozan Cream?

Monozan is a brand name for monobenzone 20% cream manufactured by Behvazan Pharmaceutical Co., based in Rasht, Iran. Monozan cream contains monobenzone (monobenzyl ether of hydroquinone / MBEH) as the active pharmaceutical ingredient — the same active ingredient found in the well-known Benoquin cream.

Monozan is used exclusively for permanent depigmentation in vitiligo patients where vitiligo affects more than 50% of the body surface. It is FDA-approved active ingredient and has been used in clinical dermatology since 1952.

Monozan vs Benoquin vs Monobenzone

These are all the same treatment with different names:

  • Monobenzone — the active pharmaceutical ingredient (generic name)
  • Monozan — Behvazan Pharmaceutical Co. brand name for monobenzone 20%
  • Benoquin — discontinued ICN Pharmaceuticals brand name for monobenzone 20%
